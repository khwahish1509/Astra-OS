# Astra OS — 5-Minute Pitch Script

**Total time: ~4:45**
**Format: You talking to camera + screen recording of live demo + slides as backdrop**

---

> That's not a bad day. That's every day when you're a solo founder.

> My name is Khwahish, and I built Astra OS — an AI Chief of Staff that runs your startup with you."

---

## [SLIDE 2 — THE PROBLEM] (0:25 – 1:00)

> "Here's the real problem: founders don't lack tools. We're drowning in them. Gmail, Calendar, Slack, Notion, your CRM, Google Drive — that's 12 apps open at any given time. And the result? We spend 3 and a half hours every day just *managing* those tools instead of building our product.

> But the worst part isn't the time lost. It's what falls through the cracks. 68% of follow-ups get missed because no single system connects your conversations to your calendar to your task list. You had a great call with an investor, you said you'd send the deck by Friday — and by Friday, you've forgotten because there was no system to remember it for you.

> That's the gap. And that's what Astra OS fills."

---

## [SLIDE 3 — THE SOLUTION] (1:00 – 1:40)

> "Astra OS replaces those 12 open tabs with one voice conversation.

> You talk to Astra the way you'd talk to a real chief of staff. It can see your screen through Gemini's multimodal vision. It hears you in real-time with native audio streaming — no speech-to-text middleman. It speaks back to you with natural voice. It remembers every decision through a persistent Company Brain. And it acts — with 60 integrated voice tools across Gmail, Calendar, Drive, Tasks, and Contacts.

> Five modalities: See, Hear, Speak, Remember, Act — all running simultaneously in a single Gemini session.

> And here's the thing that matters: zero third-party AI. One Google API key. One model. One WebSocket connection. That's it."

> "Let me show you what this actually looks like."

**[Switch to live demo — screen share of Astra OS running]**

> Now watch this — I interrupt Astra mid-sentence with a new question. See how it stops immediately and processes my new input? That's native barge-in. Gemini's voice activity detection handles it naturally, no wake word, no awkward pauses.

> And here's the part I'm most proud of: I share my screen, and I ask Astra 'What do you see?' It analyzes what's on my screen in real-time using Gemini's multimodal vision. No other consumer voice AI does this."

## DELIVERY TIPS

1. **Energy**: You're a founder, not a student presenting a school project. Speak with conviction. You built something real.
2. **Demo**: The live demo is your biggest weapon. Practice it until it's smooth. If something breaks, acknowledge it and keep going — judges respect composure.
3. **Pace**: You have 5 minutes max. This script runs ~4:45 to give you buffer. Don't rush.
4. **Eye contact**: Look at the camera when talking. Only look at your screen during the demo section.
5. **Opening**: The first 15 seconds decide if judges pay attention. The "picture this" opening pulls them into a relatable scenario immediately.
6. **Close strong**: End with the tagline and a direct "Thank you." Don't trail off.
