## [0:30–1:30] THE SOLUTION — Astra OS (60s)

*(Screen recording of Astra OS dashboard + voice session)*

"Astra OS is an AI Chief of Staff. You talk to it like you'd talk to a trusted executive — naturally, in full sentences, interrupting when you want.

Say 'Brief me' — and Astra checks your calendar, scans your inbox, reviews overdue commitments, identifies at-risk relationships, and delivers a 30-second executive briefing. All by voice. All in real-time.

*(Show email intelligence view)*

It reads every email in your inbox — not summaries, the actual emails. It classifies them by urgency, routes them to the right person, and drafts replies in your voice. When an investor emails about your Series A timeline, Astra drafts the response, sends it, and creates a follow-up task — from one voice command.

*(Show CRM / relationship view)*

Every person you interact with gets a health score. Astra tracks tone across emails, monitors response times, counts open commitments, and alerts you before a relationship declines.

*(Show task board)*

Full task management by voice. 'Create a task for Arjun to fix the onboarding bug, high priority, due Friday.' Done.

This isn't a chatbot. This is an operating system for running a startup."

## [1:30–2:30] THE INNOVATION — What Makes This Different (60s)

*(Direct to camera + architecture diagram)*

"Three things make Astra fundamentally different from every AI assistant on the market.

**First — it's voice-native, not voice-bolted.** Astra runs on Gemini 2.5 Flash with Native Audio. No speech-to-text. No text-to-speech middleware. The AI hears raw audio and speaks raw audio over a single WebSocket. That's why it feels like a real conversation — you can interrupt it mid-sentence and it handles it naturally.

**Second — the Company Brain.** Every conversation is automatically distilled into structured knowledge — commitments, decisions, risks, action items. These are embedded and stored in a vector database. When you ask 'What did we decide about pricing last week?', Astra searches semantically across every past conversation and surfaces the exact decision. It's not memory — it's institutional knowledge that grows with your company.

**Third — proactive intelligence.** Two background AI agents run 24/7. One scans your email every 60 seconds, extracting commitments and risk signals. The other monitors for overdue items, declining relationships, and blocked tasks. When something needs attention, Astra surfaces it in your next conversation — without being asked."
